# Model Categories

## Architectural Models
- Buildings (houses, offices, towers)
- Interior spaces (rooms, furniture layouts)
- Structural elements (beams, columns, walls)
- Roofs (flat, pitched, complex)
- Doors, windows, stairs

## Mechanical Parts
- Gears (spur, helical, bevel)
- Bearings and bushings
- Shafts and axles
- Fasteners (bolts, nuts, washers, screws)
- Brackets and mounts
- Housings and enclosures

## Consumer Products
- Bottles and containers
- Furniture (tables, chairs, shelves)
- Tools and handles
- Appliances
- Electronics enclosures
- Packaging

## Vehicles & Transportation
- Cars (bodies, wheels, chassis)
- Bikes and motorcycles
- Aircraft components
- Ships and boats
- Trains

## Industrial Equipment
- Pipes and fittings
- Valves and connectors
- Pumps and motors
- Frames and structures
- Conveyor systems
- Machinery parts

## Organic & Complex Forms
- Characters and figures
- Sculptures
- Terrain and landscapes
- Natural objects (rocks, trees)
- Artistic pieces

## Precision Engineering
- Threads (ISO, UNC, UNF)
- Splines and keys
- Cams and linkages
- Springs
- Precision fittings
- Measurement tools

## Parametric Models
- Configurable dimensions
- Driven by formulas
- Feature-based modeling
- Design variations
- Assembly constraints
